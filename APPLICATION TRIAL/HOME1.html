About Us

THINKover Mobile Application offers a one of a kind counseling service. It is unique and distinct for the reason that it is very rare for a company to cater the well being of clients. The mobile application is a proposal that will accommodate clients who feel unmotivated whether in academics, career or personal life. This mobile application is an easy access app with an affordable subscription fees. Downloading of mobile application is free so the customers will only pay for their subscription that varies whether they will choose gold or platinum membership and will be availing the counseling service on a daily, weekly or monthly basis. Signing in to the mobile application will give an access where they can contact a professional help via personal emails, they can also communicate through groups with the same interest, receive a daily motivational quotes or feature topics that will brighten up the day. The clients can avail the service anytime, and wherever they are for its operation is 24/7.​ ​
